Jaipur

The “pink city” of Jaipur is a much loved destination for ... those getting married due to the pure range and variety of wedding venues accessible. In Jaipur have so many luxury, heritage places as well so many Old mansions to be the perfect venue.

Jodhpur

Jodhpur is situated on the periphery of the Thar Desert. Jodhpur, ... the blue city of Rajasthan is the second largest and highly fortified city in Rajasthan. Besides, it is also an ideal venue for the Palace Wedding in Jodhpur. You can have a high time on your dream day at the graciously magnificent places at the very heart of deserts.

Udipur

Udaipur such a standout destination for a wedding in India. Udaipur is a beautiful city built around three azure lakes called Pichola, Fateh Sagar, and Udai Sagar. It is nestled in the green Aravalli hills. Foremost is the setting. Known as the city of lakes and palaces, Udaipur really is one of the most romantic and charming places in India. The Mewar royal family pioneered the concept of destination weddings in India and has been organizing them for over a decade.
